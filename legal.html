Privacy Policy

Introduction Data Collection Data Usage Your Rights Cookies

1. Introduction

At MoonMC (moonmcnet.falix.gg), we prioritize the privacy and security of our community. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information and gaming data to ensure a safe and transparent experience.

By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the essential information required to provide and maintain our Minecraft server services:

  • Account Identifiers: Minecraft Username, UUID, and Discord ID (if linked).
  • Technical Data: IP Address (for security and anti-cheat), client version, and connection logs.
  • Gameplay Data: In-game chat logs, command history, player statistics, and virtual inventory data.
  • Third-Party Data: Transaction IDs from payment processors (Tebex/PayPal) for purchase verification.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for operational purposes:

  • Service Provision: Managing your player profile and virtual assets.
  • Security: Monitoring for hacking, exploits, and rule violations.
  • Support: Resolving technical issues and player disputes via our Discord ticket system.
  • Compliance: Fulfilling legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service.

4. Your Rights

Under global data protection laws (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data associated with your UUID.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, please open a "Data Request" ticket on our official Discord.

5. Cookie Policy

Our website uses essential cookies to maintain your session and improve site performance. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
